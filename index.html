About Us

At TerraAtom, we believe in the power of technology to transform businesses and drive growth. Founded in 2024, TerraAtom was born from a shared vision among a group of passionate Dynamics 365, Business Central and Business Intelligence experts who came together in 2016. Since then, we have grown into a global consultancy firm, specializing in Dynamics 365 CRM and Salesforce solutions.
