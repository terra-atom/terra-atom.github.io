About Us

our story

At TerraAtom, we believe in the power of technology to transform businesses and drive growth. Founded in 2018, TerraAtom was born from a shared vision among a group of passionate Dynamics 365 developers who came together in 2016. Since then, we have grown into a global consultancy firm, specializing in Dynamics 365 CRM and Salesforce solutions.
